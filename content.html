Everything about EXIFrenameX

With EXIFrenameX, you bring order to any media chaos. Photos and videos are automatically renamed based on their creation date and time — at the click of a button, for thousands of files at once.

Features at a Glance

Batch Rename
Rename thousands of photos and videos in seconds. Unmatched efficiency for large media collections.
Metadata Driven
Extracts EXIF, creation date and other metadata. Never sort files manually again.
Preview & Undo
Live preview before renaming and instant undo, in case you change your mind.
Flexible Formats
Custom naming templates: prefix, suffix, original name, date, sequence — total control.
Modern UI
Dark/Light mode, drag & drop, 100% desktop-optimized. Intuitive for beginners, powerful for professionals.
Open Source
100% transparent and open code on GitHub. Security and transparency, guaranteed.

Supported File Formats

JPEG PNG TIFF Sony RAW (.ARW) Nikon RAW (.NEF, .NRW) Canon RAW (.CR2, .CR3) Olympus RAW (.ORF) Panasonic RAW (.RW2) Leica RAW (.RWL) Samsung RAW (.SRW) QuickTime (.MOV) MPEG-4 (.MP4) ...and more
More formats are added regularly — suggestions welcome via GitHub Issues.
