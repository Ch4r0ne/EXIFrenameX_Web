Why EXIFrenameX?

As a system engineer handling global data and endless media chaos, I built EXIFrenameX for efficiency. After sorting 60,000+ media files for personal projects, it was clear: automation is key. That's why EXIFrenameX is open source, cleanly engineered and available for everyone.
